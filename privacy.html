Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-15

This policy explains what is stored and processed when you use the Setfeed app and the Setfeed web bridge (setfeed.app). The goal is minimal data, explicit boundaries, and ciphertext-only transport.

Message content

Messages sent through setfeed.app are encrypted in your browser before they reach our servers. We store encrypted ciphertext only and cannot read message contents.

Private encryption keys are stored only on recipient devices and are never uploaded to our servers.
