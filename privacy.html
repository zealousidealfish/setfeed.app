Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-15

Setfeed (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what Setfeed stores and processes when you use the Setfeed app and the Setfeed web bridge (setfeed.app).

Message content

Setfeed is designed to be local-first and end-to-end encrypted. When you send a message through setfeed.app, the message is encrypted in your browser before it reaches our servers. We store encrypted ciphertext only and cannot read message contents.

Private encryption keys are stored only on recipient devices and are never uploaded to our servers.

Information we store

To operate Setfeed, we store the minimum needed for scheduling, delivery, and abuse prevention:

  • Encrypted message ciphertext and encryption metadata (for example, key version).
  • Delivery metadata (timestamps, message identifiers, and routing to the recipient).
  • Authentication state for signed sends (Secure and Personal), such as a Firebase user ID.
  • Abuse-prevention signals like rate-limit counters and hashed operational identifiers.
  • Device delivery tokens (for example, an FCM token) needed to notify a recipient device.

Information we do not store

  • Message plaintext.
  • Recipient private keys.
  • Content-based advertising profiles.

How we use information

  • To deliver messages at the time you chose (or at the recipient’s preferred time window for inbound).
  • To validate receive codes and enforce usage limits (for example, Rolling single-use).
  • To provide notifications you have enabled.
  • To maintain reliability, performance, and safety (including abuse prevention).

Rolling codes

Rolling codes are designed for temporary sharing. They may allow sending without sign-in. To prevent abuse, we apply strict constraints such as short validity windows, single-use limits, App Check validation, and rate limiting.

Third-party services

Setfeed uses Firebase services to operate the web bridge and delivery pipeline, including: Authentication, Cloud Functions, Firestore, Cloud Messaging, and App Check. These services process limited technical data required to provide their functionality.

Retention

The web bridge is a transport layer. Encrypted ciphertext may be retained for a limited period to support delivery and retries. The Setfeed app is the primary long-term store for messages on your device.

Your choices

  • You control notifications through app settings and system settings.
  • You can cancel scheduled items and remove items from your feed within the app.
  • You can avoid using setfeed.app and send only through in-app flows if preferred.
