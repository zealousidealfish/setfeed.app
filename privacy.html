Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-13

Setfeed (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Setfeed handles information when you use the Setfeed mobile application.

Information we handle

  • Messages you create and schedule.
  • Scheduling metadata (delivery time, status, and related app state).
  • Notification preferences and delivery settings.
  • Basic device/app information required for functionality and stability.
  • Receive codes used to validate inbound messages (when applicable).

How we use information

  • To schedule and deliver messages at the time you chose.
  • To enable inbound message receipt and validation.
  • To provide notifications you’ve enabled.
  • To maintain app reliability, performance, and safety.

Data storage

Setfeed is currently designed to be calm and local-first. Depending on features you use, data may be stored on your device. If network features are enabled in the future, data may be processed via server infrastructure to provide delivery and syncing.

Third-party services

Setfeed may rely on platform services such as Google Play Services. If push notifications or analytics are used, they may be provided by services like Firebase. These services may collect technical data needed to provide their functionality.

Retention and deletion

Messages remain available until you cancel or delete them within the app. You can also remove the app to stop local storage.

Your choices

  • You can control notifications in app settings and system settings.
  • You can cancel scheduled items and remove items from your feed within the app.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, contact: support@setfeed.app
