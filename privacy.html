Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-13

Setfeed (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Setfeed handles information when you use the Setfeed mobile application.

Information we handle

  • Messages you create and schedule.
  • Scheduling metadata (delivery time, status, and related app state).
  • Notification preferences and delivery settings.
  • Basic device/app information required for functionality and stability.
  • Receive codes used to validate inbound messages (when applicable).

How we use information

  • To schedule and deliver messages at the time you choose.
  • To enable inbound message receipt and validation.
  • To provide notifications you’ve enabled.
  • To maintain app reliability, performance, and safety.

Data storage

Setfeed is currently designed to be calm and local-first. Depending on features you use, data may be stored locally on your device.

If network features are introduced in the future, data may be processed via secure server infrastructure strictly to provide delivery and syncing functionality.

Third-party services

Setfeed may rely on platform services such as Google Play Services. If push notifications or analytics are used, they may be provided by services such as Firebase. These services may collect technical data necessary for functionality.

Retention and deletion

Messages remain available until you cancel or delete them within the app. You can remove the app at any time to stop local data storage.

Children’s privacy

Setfeed is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Updates will be reflected by revising the effective date above.
