Request Data Deletion

This page explains how you can request deletion of data associated with your use of Setfeed.

How to request deletion

To request deletion of backend data associated with your use of Setfeed, please email:

privacy@setfeed.app

Please include:

  • Your receive code (if applicable)
  • Approximate dates of usage
  • Any relevant context that helps identify the data

What data may be deleted

  • Encrypted message envelopes associated with your receive code (where identifiable)
  • Related backend metadata (e.g., delivery state)

What is not stored

Setfeed does not store plaintext message content when end-to-end encryption is used. Private encryption keys remain on user devices and are not uploaded to our servers.

Local device data

Data stored locally on your device (such as scheduled messages and app state) can be removed by:

  • Clearing app storage
  • Uninstalling the app

Response time

We respond to deletion requests within 30 days.
